Graph Visualization

Explore your graph in the visualization panel, click a node to see its details and click and drag to move a single or group of nodes. Here you’ll also find some quick toggles for seeing labels, and pruning isolated node (unconnected data points)



Histograms

Here, see all of your data organized by it’s attributes, create new histograms for any attribute you’d like, and apply custom color schemes for each!



Data Inspector

Explore your data in the classic format, use the data brush tool to highlight elements in the graph and see them populated here!
